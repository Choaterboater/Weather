Before reporting a problem

• Confirm the device has a network connection for live weather, maps, videos, products, and other online features.
• Confirm Location While Using, Camera, Photos, or Notifications permission is enabled if the affected feature needs it.
• Try the screen's Retry or pull-to-refresh action.
• Record the BiteCast version, iOS version, affected screen, expected result, and what happened.

Protect your privacy

Do not post precise private fishing coordinates, unredacted catch notes, API keys, account credentials, or personal contact information in a public issue. A screenshot can contain a location even when it is not obvious.

WeatherKit authentication errors

If Apple Weather reports an authentication-service error, confirm WeatherKit is enabled for the app identifier and provisioning profile, then rebuild with a current signed profile. BiteCast should fall back to the National Weather Service when that provider is available.

Console messages

Messages from PointerUI, PerfPowerTelemetry, keyboard scenes, zero-size Metal layers, or unrelated system services can be iOS framework diagnostics. Include them in a report only when they coincide with a visible BiteCast failure. A blank screen, persistent loading state, missing saved catch, wrong location, or repeatable crash is actionable.

Data removal

Remove individual catches from Catch Log. Use iOS Settings to revoke app permissions. Removing the app removes its remaining local container, subject to device or cloud backups managed through Apple.

Response and availability

The project page is the current support channel. No response time or continuous service level is promised.
